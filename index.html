Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining the IT Club.

Who can join the TSA IT Club?
Any student of TSA College with a passion for technology can join! We welcome all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced developers.
Do I need to know how to code to join?
Not at all! We have departments for Graphic Designing, Video Editing, and Event Management too. Plus, we provide workshops to help you learn coding from scratch.
Is there a membership fee?
Membership is currently free for all TSA students. Some specialized workshops or external hackathons might have nominal registration costs.
How many events do you host annually?
We host at least 5 major events every year, including our flagship Hackathon, Tech-Talks with industry experts, and monthly coding challenges.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical, industry-relevant tech skills through hands-on projects and collaborative learning. We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

The Core Philosophy

At TSA IT Club, we believe that the best way to learn technology is by building it. Traditional classroom theory is essential, but constructing real projects alongside peers is where true mastery happens.

Did you know? Over 80% of our active members secure internships before their final year, thanks to the portfolio of projects they build here.

Our Key Objectives

  • Provide hands-on coding experience through guided workshops.
  • Foster a culture of continuous learning, peer-review, and mentorship.
  • Connect students with industry professionals via seminars and networking events.
  • Promote open-source contributions and global collaboration.

What We Do

We organize various activities throughout the academic year to keep our members engaged and up-to-date with the latest tech trends. Our calendar is packed with opportunities to grow.

Technical Workshops

Every week, our senior members and guest speakers host deep-dive sessions. Topics include:

  • Modern Web Development (React, Node.js, Next.js)
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning basics
  • Cybersecurity fundamentals and capture-the-flag (CTF) strategies
  • Cloud computing and deployment (AWS, Docker)

Competitions & Hackathons

We host the annual TSA Hackathon, a 48-hour coding marathon where students solve real-world problems. We also organize monthly algorithmic coding challenges to prepare students for technical interviews.

Join our Slack channel to participate in the "Bug of the Week" bounty and win club merchandise!

Our Vision

To be the leading hub for technological innovation and excellence within the TSA College campus and beyond, producing top-tier tech talent ready for the global market.

Looking to the Future

We see a future where every student, regardless of their background, has the resources, mentorship, and community support to turn their innovative ideas into reality. By 2030, we aim to establish a fully-funded student startup incubator.

Community Impact

  • Building software solutions for local college departments.
  • Hosting tech-literacy campaigns for non-engineering students.
  • Creating a permanent alumni network for ongoing career support.

Join the Vision: We are actively looking for visionary students to lead our upcoming AI Research Division. Contact the core committee if interested!
